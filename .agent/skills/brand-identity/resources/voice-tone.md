# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text for Laapak, adhere to this brand persona.

## Brand Personality Keywords

* **Friendly & Modern**: Use welcoming language (e.g., "اهلاً بيك").
* **Reliable & Trusted**: Clear communication regarding device care and warranty.
* **Simple**: Jargon-free instructions for users.
* **Bilingual**: Support both Arabic (primary) and English (secondary).

## Grammar & Mechanics Rules

* **Arabic First**: All user-facing text should be provided in Arabic by default.
* **RTL Support**: Ensure layout and text direction are correctly handled.
* **Headings**: Use `HeadlineMedium` for page titles and `TitleMedium` for sections.
* **Clarity**: Keep sentences concise and action-oriented.

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use       | Use Instead (Arabic) | Usage |
|------------------|----------------------|-------|
| "Submit"         | "تأكيد" / "إرسال"    | Form actions |
| "Loading"        | "جاري التحميل..."    | Progress states |
| "Error"          | "حدث خطأ ما"        | Error handling |
| "Hello"          | "اهلاً بيك"          | Greeting |
| "Warranty"       | "الضمان"             | Document/State |
